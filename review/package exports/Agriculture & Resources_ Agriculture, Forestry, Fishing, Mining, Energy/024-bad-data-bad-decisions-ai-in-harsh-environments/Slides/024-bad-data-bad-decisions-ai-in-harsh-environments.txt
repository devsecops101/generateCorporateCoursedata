Slide 2 (Slide 2)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Common Sensor Failure Modes

Subtitle: How sensors break down in harsh conditions

Content:
• Drift: readings slowly shift over time
• Signal noise from electrical interference
• Complete dropouts and communication loss
• Physical damage from weather and vibration
• Harsh conditions accelerate all failures

Slide 3 (Slide 3)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: AI's Blind Trust Problem

Subtitle: Why AI can't detect bad data on its own

Content:
• AI accepts all sensor input as valid
• No built-in quality checks
• Cannot distinguish real from corrupted signals
• Trained on clean lab data, not messy field conditions
• Missing context to question unrealistic readings

Slide 4 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Weather Interference Effects

Subtitle: How environmental conditions corrupt field data

Content:
• Rain and moisture cause electrical faults
• Temperature extremes affect sensor accuracy
• Wind and vibration create false readings
• Dust and debris block or damage sensors
• Electromagnetic storms disrupt wireless signals

Slide 5 (Slide 5)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: The Cascade Effect

Subtitle: How single corrupted signals spread through systems

Content:
• One faulty sensor affects thousands of data points
• Bad readings train models incorrectly
• Predictions drift silently in wrong direction
• Small errors compound into major mistakes
• Problem often unnoticed until damage is done

Slide 6 (Slide 6)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Prevention Strategies

Subtitle: Building resilience against data corruption

Content:
• Cross-check readings from multiple sensors
• Apply smoothing to filter sudden spikes
• Install redundant sensors as backup
• Train AI on degraded data scenarios
• Set confidence thresholds for uncertain outputs

Slide 7 (Slide 7)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Recovery and Cleanup

Subtitle: Restoring integrity after corruption occurs

Content:
• Identify corrupted data sources first
• Use backup streams to fill gaps
• Flag suspect records, don't delete them
• Retrain models on cleaned datasets
• Document all recovery steps thoroughly

Slide 8 (Slide 8)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Key Takeaways

Subtitle: Essential principles for reliable AI in harsh environments

Content:
• Always question your data sources
• Context matters more than raw numbers
• Build in human oversight points
• Start small and verify before scaling
• Plan for sensor failures from day one
