Title: When AI Predictions Fail

Subtitle: The reality of misplaced confidence

Content:
• AI predictions sometimes fail
• Equipment breaks unexpectedly despite good forecasts
• High confidence scores can mislead maintenance teams
• Unplanned downtime costs escalate rapidly
• Verification strategies are essential

Slide 2 (Slide 2)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Common Causes of Model Failures

Subtitle: Why predictions go wrong

Content:
• Faulty or miscalibrated sensors feeding bad data
• Rare faults missing from training datasets
• Equipment aging beyond model experience
• Seasonal changes catching systems off guard
• Over-reliance on single sensor readings

Slide 3 (Slide 3)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Reading Early Warning Signs

Subtitle: Detecting problems before visible failure

Content:
• Subtle shifts in vibration, temperature, or sound appear days before visible faults
• Establish baseline data for normal operating ranges
• Monitor trends over time, not just threshold crossings
• Small deviations signal early component wear
• Continuous sensor monitoring catches what inspections miss

Slide 4 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Practical Verification Strategies

Subtitle: Cross-checking before you act

Content:
• Cross-reference predictions with recent sensor data
• Review model's historical accuracy on similar equipment
• Spot-check high-priority alerts manually
• Set clear action thresholds versus investigation triggers
• Consider confidence scores in context of equipment familiarity

Slide 5 (Slide 5)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Financial Impact of Missed Failures

Subtitle: When trust becomes costly

Content:
• Emergency repairs cost significantly more than planned maintenance
• Unplanned downtime creates cascading production losses
• False alarms waste maintenance resources and erode team confidence
• Late warnings offer little protection against breakdown costs
• Blind trust amplifies financial exposure

Slide 6 (Slide 6)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Diagnosing Model Performance Issues

Subtitle: Identifying patterns in prediction failures

Content:
• Data drift occurs when current conditions don't match training data
• Spikes in false negatives indicate model degradation
• Clustered failures in asset types reveal training gaps
• Seasonal variations expose model blind spots
• Overconfidence patterns highlight sensor dependency issues

Slide 7 (Slide 7)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Learning From Prediction Errors

Subtitle: Turning failures into improvements

Content:
• Wrong predictions reveal model limitations and data gaps
• Failed alerts provide valuable retraining opportunities
• Error patterns guide sensor placement and calibration
• Bias in training data creates predictable blind spots
• Systematic failure analysis strengthens future predictions

Slide 8 (Slide 8)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Building Reliable AI-Human Partnership

Subtitle: Smart strategies for sustainable success

Content:
• Treat AI outputs as probabilities, not certainties
• Pair model predictions with human judgment
• Monitor data quality continuously
• Start with small implementations and expand gradually
• Build trust through verified results, not assumptions
• Use failures as learning opportunities for model improvement
