Title: Recognising the Warning Signs

Subtitle: When AI signals need human attention

Content:
• Overly confident outputs from incomplete data
• Repeated errors in the same area
• Results contradicting known facts or policies
• Sudden shifts in tone or logic
• System flags uncertainty or incomplete responses
• Your gut feeling that something is off

Subtitle: Red flags that demand immediate human review

• Advice contradicting recent field experience
• Outputs that seem suspiciously perfect
