Title: Data Quality Problems

Subtitle: When Poor Input Data Creates Financial Forecast Failures

Content:
• Garbage in, garbage out principle applies directly to AI models
• Outdated training data produces forecasts based on obsolete conditions
• Incomplete datasets create blind spots in model predictions
• Data gaps from system migrations or missing records skew results
• Quality issues multiply when models rely on flawed foundation data

Slide 4 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Historical Bias in Training Data

Subtitle: How Past Inequalities Shape Current AI Predictions

Content:
• Historical data reflects past economic inequalities and market distortions
• Models trained on biased samples produce systematically unfair outcomes
• Certain demographics, regions, or business sectors become underrepresented
• Boom-period training data fails to predict downturn risks accurately
• Bias often remains invisible until financial damage occurs

Slide 5 (Slide 5)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Model Drift and Degradation

Subtitle: When AI Performance Deteriorates Over Time

Content:
• AI models lose accuracy as real-world conditions change from training scenarios
• Market shifts make historical patterns less relevant for current predictions
• Models become overconfident in stable conditions, underestimate volatility
• Performance degradation happens gradually, making detection difficult
• Without regular retraining, models provide false confidence in outdated assumptions

Slide 6 (Slide 6)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Implementing Human Oversight Controls

Subtitle: Essential Review Processes for AI-Generated Financial Data

Content:
• Require human review before acting on AI-generated forecasts
• Establish clear escalation procedures for unusual AI predictions
• Maintain detailed audit trails for all AI outputs and decisions
• Set accuracy thresholds that trigger manual verification
• Train staff to recognize unreliable AI outputs and red flags

Slide 7 (Slide 7)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Model Management Best Practices

Subtitle: Ongoing Strategies to Maintain AI Reliability

Content:
• Test models regularly against historical data to verify accuracy
• Retrain models frequently with current, representative data
• Document all model assumptions and limitations clearly
• Cross-validate AI outputs with independent data sources
• Rotate model versions to prevent over-reliance on single approaches
