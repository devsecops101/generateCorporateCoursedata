Subtitle: When familiar tools become unfamiliar risks

• Compliance gaps emerge silently

Title: Common Risk Sources

Subtitle: Where vulnerabilities hide in plain sight

Content:
• Free service tiers with data-sharing clauses

Slide 6 (Slide 6)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Early Warning Signs

Subtitle: Catching problems before they escalate

Content:
• Unexpected data flows leaving your systems
• New AI features appearing without notice
• Staff sharing sensitive information in prompts
• Incomplete or missing audit trail records
• Vendor policy changes in routine communications

Slide 7 (Slide 7)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Practical Risk Reduction

Subtitle: Steps to protect your organisation

Content:
• Audit existing SaaS tools for AI features
• Locate and configure AI opt-out settings
• Limit data shared with external processors
• Train staff on AI-related data risks
• Monitor vendor privacy policy updates

Slide 8 (Slide 8)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Taking Action Today

Subtitle: Start small, stay consistent

Content:
• Choose one current tool to examine closely
• Review its recent feature additions and policy changes
• Look for AI opt-out options in account settings
• Document what data it processes and where it goes
• Schedule regular check-ins rather than annual audits
