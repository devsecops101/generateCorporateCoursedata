Slide 1 (Slide 1)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: What Happens to Your Data

Subtitle: After you hit submit

Content:
• Your data doesn't always disappear when you close the chat
• AI tools often store, log, or review what you type
• Inputs can be used to improve future versions of the tool
• Personal details you share may be retained longer than you expect
• Different tools have very different data policies

Slide 2 (Slide 2)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Data Processing Pipeline

Subtitle: Behind the scenes operations

Content:
• Temporary server storage during processing
• Session logging for activity records
• Internal staff may review queries for safety
• Privacy settings can modify data handling
• Processing varies by provider terms

Slide 3 (Slide 3)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Training Data Integration

Subtitle: How your inputs improve AI systems

Content:
• User conversations become training material
• AI learns patterns and preferences from real inputs
• Your phrasing and details can be permanently incorporated
• Models improve by studying user interactions
• Training data shapes how AI responds to future users

Slide 4 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Retention Duration Risks

Subtitle: When temporary becomes permanent

Content:
• Some data inclusion can be permanent in models
• Sensitive information may persist indefinitely
• No visibility into long-term storage practices
• Deletion requests may not remove training data
• Retention policies vary significantly between tools

Slide 5 (Slide 5)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Corporate Information Exposure

Subtitle: Business data vulnerability

Content:
• Client names and confidential details at risk
• Financial figures may be retained and reused
• Project information can leak beyond your organization
• Competitive intelligence inadvertently shared
• Regulatory compliance implications

Slide 6 (Slide 6)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Privacy Control Strategies

Subtitle: Taking charge of your data

Content:
• Review and adjust privacy settings first
• Use anonymous or generic examples
• Opt out of training data programs where available
• Regularly delete chat histories
• Avoid sharing personal identifiers unnecessarily

Slide 7 (Slide 7)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Safe Usage Guidelines

Subtitle: Best practices for AI interaction

Content:
• Never enter passwords or authentication details
• Avoid real names, addresses, and contact information
• Use test data instead of actual business information
• Check default settings before first use
• Consider offline alternatives for sensitive tasks

Slide 8 (Slide 8)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Action Plan

Subtitle: Your next steps

Content:
• Audit AI tools you currently use
• Read privacy policies before adopting new tools
• Adjust data-sharing settings on existing platforms
• Establish clear guidelines for what never gets shared
• Train your team on these principles
