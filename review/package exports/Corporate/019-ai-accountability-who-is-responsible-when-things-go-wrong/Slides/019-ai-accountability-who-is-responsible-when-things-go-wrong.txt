Title: Three Levels of AI Accountability

Subtitle: How responsibility is distributed

Content:
• Employees: Use AI tools responsibly and report issues
• Managers: Oversee AI use and review outputs
• Leaders: Set policies and governance frameworks
• Shared responsibility with distinct roles
• No one person carries all the accountability

Title: The AI Accountability Ecosystem

Subtitle: Understanding all stakeholders involved

Content:
• Internal stakeholders: employees, managers, leaders
• External stakeholders: vendors and regulators
• Each group has specific responsibilities
• Problems require coordinated response
• Multiple layers of oversight and enforcement

Slide 4 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Employee Responsibilities

Subtitle: Your role as an AI user

Content:
• Follow organizational AI policies and guidelines
• Use AI tools only for approved purposes
• Report unusual outputs or concerning behaviors
• Maintain awareness of AI's role in your work
• Seek guidance when uncertain about proper use

Slide 5 (Slide 5)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Manager Oversight Duties

Subtitle: Supervising AI use within teams

Content:
• Ensure team understands AI policies
• Review AI-assisted work before approval
• Monitor team's AI usage patterns
• Provide clear guidance on tool selection
• Take accountability for team's AI-related outcomes

------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Leadership Governance Role

Subtitle: Setting organizational AI standards

Content:
• Establish comprehensive AI policies
• Define acceptable use parameters
• Create reporting and escalation procedures
• Allocate resources for AI training
• Ensure compliance with regulations

Title: Reporting Concerns and Escalation

Subtitle: Speaking up when problems arise

Content:
• Report issues early to prevent escalation
• Start with immediate supervisor
• Use formal reporting channels when available
• Escalate unaddressed concerns upward
• Document observations for investigation
