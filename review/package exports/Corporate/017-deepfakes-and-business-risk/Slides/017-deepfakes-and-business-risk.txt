Slide 2 (Slide 2)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Deepfakes Explained

Subtitle: What they are and how they work

Content:
• AI-generated videos or audio clips that replace one person's face or voice with another's
• Created using machine learning trained on existing footage and recordings
• Convincing enough to fool colleagues, clients, and security systems
• Getting easier and cheaper to make
• No specialist equipment required, just a laptop and freely available software

Slide 3 (Slide 3)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: How Criminals Use Deepfakes

Subtitle: Common attack methods targeting businesses

Content:
• CEO fraud: fake video calls authorising wire transfers
• Voice cloning for phone-based financial scams
• Fabricated conference calls to manipulate stock prices
• Fake customer service calls to extract sensitive data
• Impersonating suppliers to redirect payments

Slide 4 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Spotting the Warning Signs

Subtitle: Technical clues that reveal a fake

Content:
• Unnatural blinking patterns or stiff, limited facial movement
• Blurry or shifting edges around the hairline and face
• Audio that feels slightly out of sync with lip movements
• Odd lighting inconsistencies
• Skin texture that looks too smooth or waxy
• Background details that blur, warp, or repeat unnaturally

Slide 5 (Slide 5)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Prevention Through Policy

Subtitle: Organisational safeguards and controls

Content:
• Establish clear escalation procedures for financial requests
• Require dual approval for transfers above threshold amounts
• Limit publicly available audio and video of key personnel
• Create written confirmation requirements for sensitive decisions
• Implement access controls that prevent single-person authorisation

Slide 6 (Slide 6)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Verification Techniques

Subtitle: How to confirm authenticity in real-time

Content:
• Always verify unexpected requests through a second, trusted channel
• Establish code word systems for high-risk communications
• Ask specific questions only the real person would know
• Request video calls for voice-only requests
• Use predetermined callback numbers, never numbers provided in the request
