Title: AI's Fundamental Limitations

Subtitle: Understanding where machines fall short

Content:

• Misses cultural and situational context

• Struggles with complex ethical dilemmas
• Operates in black and white, not nuanced gray

• Responding to what isn't said out loud

Title: When AI Errors Devastate Lives

Subtitle: Real consequences of automated mistakes

Content:
• Wrongly denied loans pushing families into poverty
• Medical misdiagnoses leading to incorrect treatment

Title: Why Humans Feel Responsibility Differently

Subtitle: The weight of personal accountability

Content:
• Humans live with consequences of their choices
• Personal values shape decision priorities
• Experience teaches us what truly matters
• We can explain our reasoning face-to-face
• Accountability requires someone to answer for outcomes
