Title: Lessons From Real Failures

Subtitle: What past incidents teach us today

Content:
• The 2009 Air France 447 crash showed how automation confusion can overwhelm even trained crews under pressure
• Sensor failures matter
• Therac-25, a radiation therapy machine, killed patients when a software race condition removed its safety checks
• Over-reliance on automated alerts caused the 2018 Boeing 737 MAX MCAS system to override pilot inputs repeatedly
• Small software bugs in safety-critical systems can cascade into catastrophic, irreversible outcomes
• Real incidents share a common thread, which is that human oversight was reduced just when it was needed most
