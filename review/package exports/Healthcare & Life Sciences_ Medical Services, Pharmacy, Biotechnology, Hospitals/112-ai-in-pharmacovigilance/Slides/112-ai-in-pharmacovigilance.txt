Title: What is Pharmacovigilance?

Subtitle: The Science of Medicine Safety

Content:
• Systematic detection and assessment of drug adverse effects
• Continuous monitoring from trials to market withdrawal
• Collects reports from healthcare providers and patients
• Managed by regulatory bodies like EMA and WHO
• Essential for protecting public health

Subtitle: Why Human Review Alone Falls Short

Content:

• Critical signals can be missed or delayed
• Geographic and linguistic barriers complicate analysis
