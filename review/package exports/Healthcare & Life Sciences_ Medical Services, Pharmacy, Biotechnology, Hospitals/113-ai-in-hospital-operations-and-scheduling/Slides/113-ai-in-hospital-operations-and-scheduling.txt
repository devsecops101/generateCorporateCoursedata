Title: The Hidden Trade-offs in Optimization

Subtitle: What efficiency algorithms prioritize and ignore

Content:
• AI systems optimize for measurable metrics like throughput and cost
• Unmeasurable factors like patient comfort get deprioritized
• Algorithms assume all patients have similar needs and timeframes
• System efficiency can conflict with individual care quality
• Trade-offs are often invisible until problems emerge

Title: When Algorithms Overlook Vulnerability

Subtitle: How AI scheduling can disadvantage complex patients

Content:
• Complex patients require more time but reduce throughput metrics
• Mobility limitations ignored when assigning appointment sequences
• Multiple conditions get fragmented across separate optimized slots
• Elderly and disabled patients face the greatest scheduling barriers
• Vulnerable populations become systematically underserved

Content:
