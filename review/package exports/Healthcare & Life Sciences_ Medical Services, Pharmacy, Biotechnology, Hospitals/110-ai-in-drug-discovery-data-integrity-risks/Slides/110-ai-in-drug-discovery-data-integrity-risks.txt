Title: Data Governance and Controls

Subtitle: Foundational practices for data integrity.

Content:
• Validate training data sources before AI pipeline entry
• Implement version control for all datasets
• Separate raw data from processed outputs
• Document every data transformation step
• Establish clear audit trails for traceability
