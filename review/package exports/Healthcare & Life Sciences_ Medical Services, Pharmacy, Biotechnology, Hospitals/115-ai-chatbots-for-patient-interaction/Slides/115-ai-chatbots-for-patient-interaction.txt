• AI chatbots are everywhere now

Slide 2 (Slide 2)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Advice Accuracy Risks

Subtitle: When AI misinterprets symptoms

Content:
• AI chatbots can misinterpret symptoms and suggest the wrong level of urgency
• No ability to ask follow-up questions the way a clinician would
• Patients may act on incorrect advice without seeking further help
• Vulnerable groups (elderly, those with low health literacy) at higher risk
• A single missed red flag can have serious consequences

Slide 3 (Slide 3)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Critical Triage Failures

Subtitle: Life-threatening conditions missed by AI

Content:
• Chest pain flagged as anxiety
• Stroke symptoms deemed stress
• Sepsis missed, delays treatment
• No escalation to clinicians
• Algorithms lack clinical judgement

Slide 4 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Systemic Triage Limitations

Subtitle: Why AI triage systems fail patients

Content:
• Wrong urgency assignments delay care
• Context-dependent conditions overlooked
• No human oversight to catch errors
• Patients trust harmful recommendations
• System design lacks safety fallbacks

Slide 5 (Slide 5)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Trust Erosion Factors

Subtitle: How chatbots damage patient relationships

Content:
• Wrong information destroys credibility
• Patients feel unheard by impersonal responses
• Privacy concerns create anxiety
• Cold, robotic tone alienates users
• Errors cascade into healthcare system distrust

Slide 6 (Slide 6)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Over-reliance Dangers

Subtitle: When patients trust AI too much

Content:
• Patients bypass professional consultation
• Reassuring but wrong advice delays urgent care
• Vulnerable populations most susceptible
• Chatbots miss emotional and physical cues
• Clear boundaries must be designed upfront

Slide 7 (Slide 7)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Error Recovery Protocol

Subtitle: Steps to restore patient confidence

Content:
• Acknowledge the error promptly and without deflection
• Apologise clearly, using plain language
• Explain what went wrong
• Outline concrete prevention steps
• Offer direct access to human clinicians
• Document and report the incident properly

Slide 8 (Slide 8)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Implementation Guidelines

Subtitle: Core principles for safer AI use

Content:
• AI supports but never replaces clinical judgement
• Always verify chatbot outputs before acting
• Maintain transparency about AI involvement
• Keep humans in the loop at every stage
• Review your organisation's AI policies
• Focus on patient safety above efficiency
