Slide 4 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Spotting Low-Quality AI Output

Subtitle: Recognising the Warning Signs Early On

Content:
• Repetitive phrasing or oddly circular sentences that say the same thing twice
• Vague, confident-sounding claims with no supporting detail
• Factual errors
• Overly formal or unnatural tone that feels slightly "off"
• Generic examples that could apply to almost any topic or context
• Sudden shifts in style or accuracy mid-paragraph

------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Protecting Your Organisation's Reputation

Subtitle: Safeguarding Trust in Every AI Output

Content:
• AI outputs published under your brand name carry your organisation's credibility, not the tool's
• One misleading or offensive output can damage years of hard-won trust
• Reputation at stake
• Always review AI-generated content before it reaches customers, clients, or the public
• Establish clear internal standards for what AI output is acceptable to publish
• Treat AI content with the same scrutiny you would apply to any official communication
