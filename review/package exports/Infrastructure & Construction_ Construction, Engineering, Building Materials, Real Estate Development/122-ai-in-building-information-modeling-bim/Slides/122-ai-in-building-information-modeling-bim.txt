Title: AI and BIM: An Introduction

Subtitle: Exploring digital construction's intelligent future

Content:
• BIM creates detailed digital models of buildings before construction begins
• AI adds intelligence to these models
• Together, they help teams spot errors, predict problems, and improve accuracy across complex projects
• Used widely in UK construction and infrastructure
• Poor data quality in BIM models can lead to costly mistakes on site
• This lesson introduces how AI is changing the way we build digitally

Title: Common Data Accuracy Risks

Subtitle: Understanding threats to model reliability

Content:
• Human input errors corrupt measurements and element placement
• Design clashes between building systems cause expensive rework
• Version control failures mislead teams with outdated files
• Poor quality inputs generate unreliable outputs
• Security breaches threaten sensitive project data
