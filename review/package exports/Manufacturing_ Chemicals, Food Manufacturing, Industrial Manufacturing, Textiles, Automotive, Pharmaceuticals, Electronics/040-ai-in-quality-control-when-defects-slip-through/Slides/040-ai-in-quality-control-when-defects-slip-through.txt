Slide 6 (Slide 3)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Blind Spots in AI Vision

Subtitle: Where training gaps distort AI sight

Content:
• Training data that lacks rare defect types leaves AI unable to recognise faults it has never seen before
• Lighting changes alone can fool a model
• Surface textures that look similar to defects cause frequent false negatives in visual inspection systems
• Angles and orientations not represented in training images create consistent blind spots
• Small scratches or micro-cracks often fall below the resolution threshold the model was trained on
• Edge cases accumulate silently, meaning defects slip through without any alert or flag

Title: What to Remember

Subtitle: Core lessons for smarter AI oversight
