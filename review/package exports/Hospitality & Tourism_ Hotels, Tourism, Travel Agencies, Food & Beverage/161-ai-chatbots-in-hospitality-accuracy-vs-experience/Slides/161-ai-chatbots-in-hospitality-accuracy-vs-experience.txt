Title: When Chatbots Get It Wrong

Subtitle: Navigating accuracy, experience, and real risks

Content:
• AI chatbots are becoming common tools in hotels, restaurants, and travel services
• They handle bookings, answer questions, and guide guests
• But errors can cause real problems
• Incorrect information leads to missed reservations, wrong advice, and frustrated customers
• This course explores where things go wrong and how to reduce the risks

Title: Booking Errors and Their Costs

Subtitle: When mistakes cost time and money

Content:
• Wrong room types or dates confirmed to guests, triggering costly rebooking and compensation
• Refund disputes and staff time wasted
• Guests arriving to find no availability due to a chatbot error
• Reputational damage from negative reviews citing booking failures
• Revenue lost when incorrect pricing is quoted and honoured

Slide 5 (Slide 3)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Consequences of Getting It Wrong

Subtitle: When errors cost trust and revenue.

Content:
• A wrong booking date or room type can trigger immediate cancellation and lost revenue
• Guests who receive bad advice rarely complain quietly
• Reputational damage spreads fast via review platforms like TripAdvisor and Google
• Repeat bookings lost
• Staff time spent fixing AI errors adds hidden operational costs
• A single viral complaint can undo months of positive guest experience work

Slide 7 (Slide 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Minimising Risk Through Verification

Subtitle: Checks That Protect Guests and Businesses

Content:
• Always confirm critical booking details (dates, room type, guest count) through a secondary system before finalising
• Cross-reference chatbot responses against live availability data in real time
• Flag ambiguous requests for human review
• Train staff to spot common chatbot error patterns, such as misread dates or duplicate entries
• Keep a clear audit trail of chatbot interactions so errors can be traced and corrected quickly
• Never rely solely on chatbot output for high-value or complex reservations
