Title: Reducing Risk in Practice

Subtitle: Practical steps to minimise costly errors

Content:
• Always validate AI-generated figures against a second source before acting on them
• Build in a human review step for any decision above a set financial threshold
• Keep audit trails
• Document where AI was used in a process and what assumptions it made
• Test your AI tools regularly with known data to catch drift or degradation in accuracy
• Treat AI output as a starting point, not a final answer
